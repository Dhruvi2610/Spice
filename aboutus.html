About Us

Welcome to Nandurbari Chilli, your number one source for the finest quality chilli powder. We are dedicated to providing you with the very best of spices, with an emphasis on purity, flavor, and authenticity.

Founded in 2025 by passionate youth who believe in purity and the health of people, Nandurbari Chilli has come a long way from its beginnings on a small farm. When we first started out, our passion for organic farming drove us to start our own business.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
